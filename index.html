What is TryliusValues?

TryliusValues is a political compass test that is inspired by https://sapplyvalues.github.io You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Extremely Agree to Extremely Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.

* 4 additional answer options have been added. More importantly, all questions were replaced to lead the fight against bias in online political tests. I'm human, though, so I recognize it won't be perfect..
